# ResumeBuilder — Audience

Last synced from: `distribution-context.md` + `distribution-os/projects/resumebuilder.md` (2026-05-28)

## Acquisition Wedge

Active job seekers who need a job-tailored, ATS-aware resume fast — especially those who have tried pasting job descriptions into ChatGPT and gotten generic output. On iOS: mobile-first users who work on their resume during commute, lunch, or between interviews.

## Segments

### Segment A: Active job seeker, English (primary ICP)

- Trigger: applying to multiple roles per week; often doing it from their phone during commute or lunch break
- Lives on: LinkedIn, job boards, r/jobs, r/resumes
- Pains: every role needs a different resume, ATS rejection fear, generic AI output that sounds like everyone else
- Workarounds: Google Docs templates, paid resume services, ChatGPT prompts
- Words they use: "ATS resume", "tailor my resume", "resume template", "applicant tracking", "beat the ATS"
- iOS use pattern: paste job posting on phone, export PDF before the interview

### Segment B: Career switcher

- Trigger: pivoting industries or roles
- Lives on: LinkedIn, Reddit career subs, career-coach newsletters
- Pains: how to translate prior experience to a new function
- Workarounds: a career coach, copying templates from new field
- Words they use: "career change resume", "career pivot", "transferable skills"

### Segment C: Polisher (existing resume, specific role)

- Trigger: applying for one or two important roles
- Lives on: LinkedIn, professional networks
- Pains: existing resume feels stale; not sure what to cut
- Workarounds: ask a friend in the industry, hire someone
- Words they use: "improve my resume", "make my resume stronger"

### Segment D: Hebrew-speaking job seeker

- Trigger: applying in the Israeli market
- Lives on: LinkedIn (he/en), Israeli job boards
- Pains: most resume tools assume US English conventions
- Workarounds: Word templates, ad hoc help
- Words they use: (Hebrew equivalents and Hebrew job titles)

## Anti-Segments

- Senior executives wanting bespoke human help — refer out
- Students with no experience — usable but not core ICP
- Recruiters / TA professionals — not the buyer
- Android users — out of scope for iOS app in 2026 (web still serves them)

## Where To Reach Each

| Segment | Best Channels |
|---|---|
| A (English active seeker) | ASO ("resume builder", "ats resume"), directories, free ATS tool web CTA, lifecycle email |
| B (career switcher) | SEO ("career change resume", "career pivot resume"), comparison pages, career coach partnerships |
| C (polisher) | ASO ("improve my resume"), programmatic SEO for specific roles |
| D (Hebrew seeker) | Hebrew ASO, Israeli job board partnerships, Hebrew landing page |
