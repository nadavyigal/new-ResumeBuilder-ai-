## 2026 AI Resume Tools to Watch

The AI resume space has expanded rapidly. "AI-powered" is no longer a differentiator — position on ATS accuracy + job-tailoring depth + Hebrew authored variant.

| Name | Angle | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Poe / ChatGPT (generic) | Prompt-based resume help | No persistence, no ATS awareness, no template parsing |
| Resume.io | Template marketplace + AI edits | Large catalog; output quality inconsistent for ATS |
| Zety | High-traffic resume builder | Template-heavy; ATS claims not deeply differentiated |
| Enhancv | Design-focused resume builder | Beautiful but ATS parsing risk |
| LinkedIn Resume Builder | Native, free | Generic; not job-tailored; no PDF/ATS audit |

## Watchlist

- LinkedIn's native resume AI features (expanding rapidly)
- Major ATS vendor changes (Workday, Greenhouse, Lever)
- New AI resume tools funded in 2026
- Apple Intelligence resume suggestions (if Apple expands writing tools)
