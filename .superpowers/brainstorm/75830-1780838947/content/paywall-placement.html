Where does the paywall sit?

The same freemium model — but three very different places to draw the line. Each changes what the free user experiences before they decide to pay.

A

Wall at export — see everything, pay to download

The full tailored resume is visible in the app. ATS score, optimized sections, templates — all readable. The wall hits only when they tap Export PDF or Copy text.

B

Wall after first use — 1 free tailoring, then pay

First optimization is completely free — full result, full export. The paywall appears the second time they try to tailor for a new job.

Strongest proof of value

  • User gets a real win before paying — no risk
  • Higher trust, easier second purchase

Risk

  • One-and-done users never trigger the paywall
  • Harder to track free vs paid cohorts
C

Wall at Expert features — tailoring free, power features paid

Tailoring + basic export are always free. The paywall is on the Expert layer: cover letter, deep ATS analysis, keyword suggestions, full section rewrites.

Highest free-tier value

  • Free tier is genuinely useful — grows user base
  • Natural upsell to power users applying to many jobs

Risk

  • Most users never need Expert — low upsell rate
  • Feels like a subscription, not a transactional purchase
