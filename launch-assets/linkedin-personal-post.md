# LinkedIn Personal Post

I got tired of watching friends send the same resume to every job and hope for the best.

Over the last few months I kept hearing the same story: "I know I am qualified, but I never hear back." That pushed me to build something small at first — a way to check whether a resume actually fits a specific job before spending an hour tailoring it.

After a lot of late nights (and more than a few false starts), Resumely is now live on the App Store. It is not perfect, but it already shows people what's missing in their resume relative to a job and helps them fix it fast.

I would love your honest feedback. If you have a resume handy, try it and tell me what felt confusing, broken, or surprisingly helpful.

Check it out here: https://apps.apple.com/app/resume-ai-cv-builder/id6776752349

If you are open to sharing feedback, I will happily return the favor on anything you are building.
