## Email Design Guidelines

### Formatting:
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences max)
- Use bullet points for scannability
- Include clear, prominent CTAs
- Use emojis sparingly for emphasis (not in every line)

### Tone:
- Friendly but professional
- Encouraging and supportive
- Educational, not salesy (until Email 5)
- Personal (use "I" and "you")

### CTAs:
- One primary CTA per email
- Button format for main CTA
- Secondary text links for supporting CTAs
- Clear, action-oriented language
